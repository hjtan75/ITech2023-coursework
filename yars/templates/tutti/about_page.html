About us

FAMILY OWNED SINCE 1977

Peter is the true inspiration and beating heart of our restaurant, having grown up in the Italian countryside of Bologna, his love of pizza making stemming from helping his grandfather with his pizzeria. in 1977, at the age of 18, with big dreams and an entrepreneurial spirit, Pete left Italy to open Tutti's Pizzeria in Canary Wharf, London. Through sheer dedication and hard work, he expanded the store and won awards for the deliciousness of his Margherita pizzas.

Living our values every day

We work constantly as a team, always supporting each other and using our initiative to make you feel plus general warmth. Everything we do is prepared with care - from team training, dish preparation, choice of dining environment and more.
